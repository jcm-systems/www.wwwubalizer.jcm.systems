WWWubalizer is now defunct

What's this then?

The WWWubalizer was a service that sent people who manually type in your apex (or "naked") domain over to your main website.

It has now been turned off.

All users who informally registered their use of the service were advised of this with plenty of time to stop using it.

If you still have naked domains pointing at 35.178.28.1, they'll be broken. Search for them in your DNS records and change them to something else.
